Who I Am

I'm Mayuresh Khare, a second-year Computer Engineering student at Lokmanya Tilak College of Engineering, specialising in AI & Data Science. I got into programming because I wanted to understand how things work — and then build things that actually work. That curiosity is still what drives most of what I do.

Over the past two years I've built projects across web development, machine learning, and data analysis — including a wildfire vegetation recovery model that hit R² 0.85, and a financial transaction analyser that helped make sense of spending patterns. I'm most comfortable when I'm learning something unfamiliar, writing code I'm not embarrassed by, and working with people who care about getting the details right.
